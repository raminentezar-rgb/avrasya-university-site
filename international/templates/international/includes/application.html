How to Complete your Application?

International students, who had graduated or are expecting to graduate from a High School equivalent to Turkish High Schools, can apply to Avrasya University. All International applicants who are applying for full-time undergraduate degree programs are required to fill out the application form and prepare all the required documents.

Step by Step Application Process

1

Apply Online

Complete the International Student Online Application Form

2

Fill Out Information

Provide all required personal and academic information

3

Send Required Documents

Copy of High School Diploma (in English), High School Transcript (in English), and Passport

4

Receive Conditional Acceptance

Get your conditional acceptance letter after document verification

5

Apply for Student Visa

If required, apply for student visa at Turkish Consulate

6

Attend AVU YÖS & Turkish Exam

Take Avrasya University's entrance examinations

7

Complete In-Person Registration

Final registration at Avrasya University with all original documents

Citizenship Criteria - Accepted Applicants

  • Foreign high school graduates or last year students
  • Natural born Turkish citizens who abandoned citizenship
  • Dual nationality students (acquired Turkish/TRNC citizenship)
  • Turkish/TRNC citizens who completed entire high school abroad
  • TRNC citizens with GCE A/L results

Citizenship Criteria - Not Accepted

  • Turkish citizens (except those with full education abroad)
  • Dual Turkish nationality (except full education abroad)
  • Dual nationality with TRNC (except with GCE A/L)
  • Students from foreign schools in Turkey
  • Students expelled from Turkish higher education institutions
Application Criteria Details

Evaluation Basis: International applicants are admitted based on high school diploma GPA, international and national examination results.

Validity Period: University entrance examinations are valid for two years from administration date. High school exit examinations have no validity limitation.

Acceptance Process: All acceptance decisions are determined by relevant admission committee and Senate approval.

How to Prepare?

Proper preparation is key to a smooth transition to student life in Turkey. Follow these guidelines to ensure you're ready for your academic journey at Avrasya University.

Visa Preparation

Student Visa Application

You will need a "student visa" to enter Turkey. Apply for the student visa at the Turkish Consulate in your country as soon as you receive your acceptance letter.

Visa Application Tips:
  • Start the visa process immediately after receiving acceptance
  • Check specific requirements of your local Turkish Consulate
  • Allow adequate time for visa processing (4-8 weeks typically)
  • Make travel arrangements considering visa processing time
  • Ensure your passport is valid for at least 2 years

Health Insurance Preparation

Important Note

While health insurance is not a visa requirement for Turkey, all international students are strongly recommended to have comprehensive international health insurance coverage.

Insurance Options to Consider:
International Health Insurance

Coverage valid in Turkey and during travel

Turkish Public Insurance (SGK)

Can be obtained after arrival in Turkey

University-Supported Insurance

Check with university for available options
