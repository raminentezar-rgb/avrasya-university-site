About Us

Avrasya University which is located in the city of Trabzon is on the Black Sea coast of north-eastern Turkey. Trabzon is a vibrant province of 786.326 and the university serves as one of the hub of academic, social and cultural activity.

Avrasya University was founded in 2010 as the only Foundation in Black Sea Region and Trabzon's second institution of higher education. With high qualified faculties and educational facilities throughout its campuses, Avrasya University today attracts many high school graduates from different regions of Turkey.

The university hosts approximately 8,500 students pursuing degrees in 26 undergraduate 14 graduate and 36 vocational programs. The medium of instruction at Avrasya University is Turkish. Together with English, Russian, Italian, Korean languages are taught in University.
