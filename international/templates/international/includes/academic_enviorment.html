Computer Center

The Avrasya Computer Center (BCC) provides a variety of computing resources and services to meet the administrative, educational and research computing requirements in the university community.

Our Services & Resources

BCC offers comprehensive computing services including:

Hardware & Software Maintenance

Regular maintenance and updates for all university computing equipment

Software Development

In-house application software development for university systems

Network Infrastructure

Comprehensive campus-wide network management and support

Security & Support

24/7 technical support and cybersecurity services

Computer Laboratories

All computers in our laboratories have NET and Internet access, providing students with the tools needed for their academic success.

High-Speed Internet Printing Facilities Cloud Storage Research Databases

Dorm Net

Avrasya University's networking facilities are extended to the dormitories as well. All campus dormitory rooms are wired for Ethernet, and students living in the dormitories can connect their own computers to NET and the Internet.

"Stay connected everywhere on campus - from classrooms to dorm rooms, we ensure seamless connectivity for all students."

International Students Office

The Office of International Students plays a significant role in the internationalization of Avrasya University by promoting its programs and providing support for all international academic affairs.

Our Mission

We provide assistance and guidance to both international degree-seeking students and incoming/outgoing exchange students through all phases of their academic and social integration.

Pre-Arrival Support

Comprehensive guidance on application processes, visa procedures, and accommodation arrangements before your arrival in Turkey.

Academic Integration

Assistance with course selection, academic advising, and understanding the Turkish higher education system.

Social & Cultural Activities

Orientation programs, cultural trips, and social events to help international students adapt to life in Turkey.

University Library

The Avrasya University Library contains a large book and periodical collection, music listening booths, CDs, records, audio and videocassettes, music scores and microfilms.

Collections & Resources

Extensive Book Collection

Over 250,000 volumes across all academic disciplines

Periodicals & Journals

Subscriptions to major international academic journals

Multimedia Resources

Audio-visual materials, music scores, and listening stations

Digital Resources

Access to leading online databases and e-journals

The Library receives periodicals from numerous institutions, scientific articles and technical reports from prominent research institutions in Europe and the USA, and subscribes to major foreign newspapers.

Library Locations & Hours

Main Library Building

Located on the Main Campus, this is our primary library facility with the most extensive collections.

Hours: Monday-Friday: 8:30 AM - 10:00 PM, Saturday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

East Campus Branch Library

Located on the upper floor of the Cafeteria Building, convenient for students on the East Campus.

Hours: Monday-Friday: 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM, Saturday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Both libraries operate on an open-stack system, allowing students direct access to all materials.
